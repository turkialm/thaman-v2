NYC · Market Overview 🗽 Stack v22
New York City
Market

THAMAN Stack v22 trained on 157,329 NYC residential sales (2022–2026), validated on 27,763 holdout transactions — newest 15% by sale date. Covers all 5 boroughs across 212 NTA geographic units.

THAMAN MODEL SIGNAL Baseline model achieves only R²=0.24, confirming NYC property pricing is highly non-linear. Stack v22 lifts to R²=0.6495 — a +0.41 R² improvement over baseline. Highest-value segments ($3M–$10M) remain hardest to predict (MedAPE=39.77%) due to unobservable co-op board decisions and bespoke interior finishes.
