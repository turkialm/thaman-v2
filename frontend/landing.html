Getting Started

Three steps to a valuation

No account, no API key, no setup. Works in your browser.

1
Pick a city
Toggle between NYC and Riyadh using the city buttons on the map. The model, layers, and form switch automatically.
2
Click the map
Drop a pin anywhere in the city boundary. Spatial features (subway distance, crime rate, income, POIs) are auto-computed from coordinates.
3
Fill in details
Enter building size, age, type, and floors. For Riyadh: area in m² and property type. Then click Estimate Price.
4
Get your estimate
Instant price with confidence interval, AVM grade, SHAP drivers, nearby sales, and location quality grid. All in under 300ms.
